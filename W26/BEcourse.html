PICT B.E. Course Structure

First Year (FE)

  • Engineering Mathematics I

    Foundation for calculus and linear algebra in engineering.

  • Engineering Physics

    Fundamentals of mechanics, optics, and electromagnetism.

  • Basic Electrical Engineering

    Basic concepts of electric circuits and machines.

  • Programming in C

    Introduction to problem-solving using C programming.

Second Year (SE)

  • Data Structures

    Understanding and implementing basic data structures.

  • Digital Electronics

    Study of logic gates, circuits, and number systems.

  • Computer Organization

    Architecture and functioning of computer systems.

  • Discrete Mathematics

    Logic, sets, combinatorics, and graph theory.

Third Year (TE)

  • Operating Systems

    Concepts of process management, memory, and scheduling.

  • Database Management Systems

    Design and querying of relational databases.

  • Computer Networks

    Fundamentals of networking models, protocols, and layers.

  • Web Technologies

    Building interactive and responsive web applications.

Final Year (BE)

  • Machine Learning

    Introduction to models and algorithms for pattern learning.

  • Cloud Computing

    Exploring scalable and on-demand computing platforms.

  • Project Work

    Team-based final year project with industrial relevance.

  • Electives

    Advanced topics such as AI, Blockchain, or Cybersecurity.
